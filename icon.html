Slize icons

Paste this cdn link to your html document: https://ubeyin.github.io/slize/1.0/icon.js

Then add this snippet to your html document:
<i    data-slize="icon_name"    data-slize-setting=' fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" '></i>

You can add extra svg codes in data-slize-setting.
